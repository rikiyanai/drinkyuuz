About

How we started

Our mission is to bring the finest quality yuzu to the U.S. in an easy-to-enjoy, ready-to-drink form. 

It all began in the remote mountain village of Kito, nestled in Japan’s Shikoku Region, where the world’s most fragrant yuzu are cultivated by passionate and dedicated growers. Experience their work and stunning surroundings virtually – we’re sure you’ll fall in love with the breathtaking landscape and their exceptional yuzu, just as we did!

​Our yuzu-ade is packed with yuzu’s delightful complexity – a burst of mandarin, the tanginess of lime, a floral touch of lemon, and the zing of grapefruit. Sweetened just right with organic cane sugar, it’s the perfect refreshment for beach days and sports adventures... 

Bring along YUUZ Yuzu-ade, Cold or Warm!

...Or, enjoy it warmed up during cooler seasons to give you a morning boost or help calm your mind at bedtime!
